# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our project and community a harassment‑free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio‑economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

* Be respectful and considerate.
* Use inclusive language.
* No harassment, intimidation, or discrimination.
* Refrain from offensive jokes or comments.
* Respect privacy and personal data.

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers will:
* Enforce this Code of Conduct.
* Take appropriate actions for violations.
* Provide a safe environment for discussion.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior to the project maintainers. Violations may result in a warning, temporary ban, or permanent removal from the project.
